About Lord Venkateswara

Lord Venkateswara, also known as Balaji, Srinivasa, and Govinda, is a form of Lord Vishnu who resides at Tirumala. Millions of devotees visit Tirumala temple every year to seek blessings.

History of Tirumala

Tirumala is one of the most sacred pilgrimage centers in India. The temple of Lord Venkateswara is located on the Tirumala hills in Andhra Pradesh and is visited by millions of devotees every year.
